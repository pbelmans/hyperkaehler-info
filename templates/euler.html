Euler characteristic

The topological Euler characteristic $\mathrm{e}(X)$ is the alternating sum of the Betti numbers of a manifold. It is also the top Chern number, so for a $2n$-dimensional hyperk√§hler manifold $X$ we are interested in $\int\mathrm{c}_{2n}(X)$.
